Privacy Policy

SpoonWise

Spoonwise does not collect, store, or sell personal user data.

Information Usage

The app allows users to enter food ingredients they have on hand. These ingredients are used only to fetch recipe suggestions from third-party recipe services. Ingredient data is not linked to any personal identity and is not stored by the app.

No Accounts or Tracking

Spoonwise does not require user accounts and does not use analytics, advertising identifiers, or tracking technologies.

Third-Party Services

Recipe suggestions are retrieved from external recipe providers. Requests may include ingredient keywords, but no personal or identifying information is transmitted.

Offline Use

Some features may work offline using cached data stored locally on the device. Cached data is not transmitted.

Children’s Privacy

Spoonwise does not knowingly collect personal information from children.
